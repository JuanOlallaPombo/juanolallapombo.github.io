I am MSc in space systems student with a growing passion for space exploration and for the combination of Machine Learning and other Artificial Intelligence techniques to the fast-paced space industry. I believe that these tools can help shape a more efficient, sustainable and versatile panorama where innovative technological solutions can thrive and be translated into benefits for society. To accomplish that, my vision is focused on research in dynamical environments that stay close to industrial partners and international institutions.

I received by Bachelor's degree from the Polytechnic University of Madrid, where I am also coursing my Master's. I have combined this with stays in foreign institutions such as the Technical University of Berlin (ERASMUS+) and the Max Planck Institute for Astronomy (master's thesis), and in top European space companies such as Satlantis or DHV Technology.

My research career has been focused on the applications of Computer Vision and Machine Learning to multiple engineering tasks, from space engineering to biomechanics. I am the first author of a peer-reviewed paper and of another one which is currently under review.
